About
D' Escuro's Tailoring

D' Escuro's Tailoring is a popular tailoring company in Nabua, Camarines Sur, Philippines which started in 1965. Founded by Delfin Espinolo Escuro Sr.

D' Escuro's Tailoring has emerged to revolutionize tailor made clothing solutions in the Philippines. The main core of its business is designing, manufacturing and wholesale/retail of tailor made clothing solutions mainly serving corporate, businesses and individual clients locally in Nabua, Iriga City, Bato and nearby city and provinces.

D' Escuro's Tailoring started its operation in San Pablo City, Laguna on 1965. On 1979, it started at its new location at Public Market Bldg #1 Stall #32, San Francisco, Nabua, Camarines Sur, Philippines to serve growing demands in Nabua Area.
